This is the Houston branch of the Perl Mongers. Perl Mongers is a loose association of international Perl User Groups.

We meet the second Tuesday of each month at HAL PC from 7PM to 9PM. Click here for directions. You don't have to be a member of HAL PC to attend. Of course, HAL PC encourages you to join.

Lately our meetings have consisted of a presentation, with plenty of audience participation. Before and after the presentation, we discuss various topics that are at least vaguely related to Perl.

We have all level of members from complete novices to very experienced Perl programmers working in Perl daily and more than willing to share their expertise.

Bring your curiosity, questions, problem code, or expertise to share.

We also have a mailing list. To join, send a message to Majordomo@linuxtx.com with "subscribe pm-houston" in the body of the message (without the quotes).
