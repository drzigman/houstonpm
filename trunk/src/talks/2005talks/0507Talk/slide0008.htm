2.6 The Differences

The main differences between these two approaches are listed below:

    The Regexp::Common approach is easier to read.
    The raw regex also matches clusters of digits without commas.
    The raw regex also matches numbers like 1234567,890.
    Extracting the numbers requires a little more work with Regexp::Common. 
    
   @nums = ($str =~ /(-?\d+(?:,\d\d\d)*)/g);

or 

    
   my $re = $RE{num}{int}{-sep => ','}{-group => 3}{-keep};
   push @nums, $1 while $str =~ /$re/g;
