5.6 Unnecessary Captures

    If you don't need the text, don't capture it.
    Capturing generates a small amount of overhead for copying and saving the matched text.
    Backtracking over a capture adds more overhead for deleting the old save.
    Use (?: ) grouping if you don't need a capture.
    Don't use $`, $&, or $', they slow down all regexes.
