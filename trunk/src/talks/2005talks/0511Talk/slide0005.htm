4.1 Other Inline Modules

Other people decided to build new Inline Language Support Modules (ILSM). Some of the supported ILSMs are:

    Inline::C
    Inline::CPP
    Inline::Java
    Inline::Python
    Inline::Ruby
    Inline::PUGS
    Inline::Guile
    etc.
