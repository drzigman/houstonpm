6.1 In the Same Regex

The matched text may be reused in the regex with \1, etc. 

    
   # match a quoted string.
   $string =~ m/(["']).*?\1/;

This matches a quoted string only if the open and close quotes are the same. Also does not handle escaped quotes in the string.
