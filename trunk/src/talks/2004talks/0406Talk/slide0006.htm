Clearifying some confusion

Most of these are pretty straightforward, but the /x and /e modifiers sometimes cause confusion. Let's look at a simple use of each of these. This first example is the routine for adding comma's to a number and can be found in the FAQ and it looks like this. 

    
	s{(\d\d\d)(?=\d)(?!\d*\.)}{$1,}g
