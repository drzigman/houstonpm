Parsing Command Line Options - Getopt::Std

The Getopt::Std module (included in the standard distribution) makes our life a great deal easier. We'll consider two kinds of options -- single letter options (like perl's -w switch) that work like switches to turn a behavior on or off, and single letter options that take arguments (like perl's -e option, which takes a string argument).
