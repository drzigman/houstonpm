Avoiding Duplicates Summary

If you look in the perlfaq4 manpage (perldoc perlfaq4) you'll find an entry for extracting the unique elements of an array with a few variations on the basic theme shown above. Obviously, the technique is general and not limited to working on arrays -- if your problem was to analyze a text document and list all of the unique words in the file sorted by their frequency, you would also want to use a hash to keep track of the unique words and the number of times they occur.
