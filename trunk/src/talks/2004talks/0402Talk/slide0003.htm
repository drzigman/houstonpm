Results for each method (on a fairly slow machine) 

    
    (using s///)
    ** fbr **
    Took 4.41 seconds on 100000 iterations

    using tr///)
    ** fbr **
    Took 1.91 seconds on 100000 iterations

So you can see that the tr/// is much faster than the s/// operator for substituting or deleting one character at a time.
