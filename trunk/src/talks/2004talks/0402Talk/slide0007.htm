Using The DATA Filehandle

Perl provides you with a special filehandle that you can use within your programs, the DATA filehandle. Reading from this filehandle will begin reading lines of data below the special __END__ or __DATA__ tokens in your program.

This is useful for testing out constructs and algorithms with test data included right in the script, or including configuration or other needed data within a script.
