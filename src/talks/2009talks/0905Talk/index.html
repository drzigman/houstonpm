SVG from Perl

SVG is an XML-based, vector graphics format specified by the World Wide Web Consortium. In May, G. Wade Johnson gave a talk on generating SVG from Perl. The presentation provides a little background on the format, in addition to discussing some of the ways that SVG can be generated by Perl.

Don't forget that the notes are available either by printing the presentation or by selecting the Ø entry on the menu at the lower right.
