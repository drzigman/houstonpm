11.2 Another Approach

    Building a regex out of smaller regexes 
    
  # A little hard to read
  my %parms = /([^=&]+)=([^=&]*)&?/g;

  # Now try built from smaller expressions.

  my $name = qr/[^=&]+/; # match a name not including '=' or field separator.
  my $value = qr/[^=&]*/; # match a (possibly empty) value.
  my $sep = qr/&?/; # match separator

  my %parms = /($name)=($value)$sep/g;
