7.2 Interpolation

Interpolation is the insertion of the value of a scalar or list inside a string, replacing its name.

    A scalar interpolates into its value: "hello, $name"
    A list interpolates into all of its values separated by the value of $" (usually a space.): "Words: @wordlist\n"
    Hashes don't interpolate.
