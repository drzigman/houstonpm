3 Why Do We Need References?

Okay, so now you know how to make references. The real question is why?

    Simplify working with an entire array or hash in a subroutine.
    Some problems in computer science are most easily solved by adding a level of indirection.
    Supports creation of complex data structures.
    Simplifies associating behavior with data.
