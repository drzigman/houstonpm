2.2 A Common Example

Let's look at a relatively simple problem. We are given a chunk of text that we want to extract all the numbers from.

The simplest solution that most people come up with looks like this: 

    
   /(\d+)/g

If you realized that there might be negative numbers, you might have done: 

    
   /(-?\d+)/g

Both of these are relatively straight-forward, matching clusters of digits.
