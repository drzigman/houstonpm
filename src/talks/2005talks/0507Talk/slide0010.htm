3.1 Methods

The patterns generated this way are objects. They support two methods: 

    
  $RE{num}{int}->matches( $str );

and 

    
  $RE{num}{int}->subs( $str => 'number' );

This does simple substitutes, but does not support use of captured text in the replacement text. (No use of $1 and friends.)
