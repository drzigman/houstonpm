Monthly Meetings

We meet the second Tuesday of each month at HAL PC from 7PM to 9PM. Click here for directions. You don't have to be a member of HAL PC to attend. Of course, HAL PC encourages you to join.

Lately our meetings have consisted of a presentation, with plenty of audience participation. Before and after the presentation, we discuss various topics that are at least vaguely related to Perl.

We have just begun our first group programming project. The January, 2006 meeting began a group programming project to control a USB-based visual indicator from Perl. When this project runs its course, we'll need to decide whether we want to do another one, go back to the lecture style, or do something else.
