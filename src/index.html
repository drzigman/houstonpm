This is the Houston branch of the Perl Mongers. Perl Mongers is a loose association of international Perl User Groups.

We meet on the second Tuesday of each month in the lobby of 3131 W. Alabama St., starting sometime between 6pm and 6:20pm. Once everyone arrives, we head upstairs to a conference room for the meeting. Check the meetings link for details.
