A Small On Line Wellness Business Built Using Perl by Fraser Baker

This is the branch of the Perl Mongers meeting in Houston, Texas. Perl Mongers is a loose association of international Perl User Groups.

We meet on the second Thursday of each month in the lobby of 3131 W. Alabama St., starting sometime between 6:30pm and 7pm. Once everyone arrives, we head upstairs to a conference room for the meeting. Check the meetings link for details.

Our meeting attendance has always been highly variable. Recently, there have been 10-20 people at the meetings.

To join our mailing list, go to the Houston.pm Mailing List page and fill out the form to subscribe.
