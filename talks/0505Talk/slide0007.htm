5.2 Non-Greedy Quantifiers

    Just like their greedy counterparts, but match as little as possible to result in a match.
      * => *? - zero or more
      + => +? - one or more
      ? => ?? - zero or one
      {n,} => {n,}? - n or more
      {n,m} => {n,m}? - at least n, but no more than m
