Read in perlfaq filenames.

We use opendir and readdir to get a list of the files in the $faqdir, and extract only those with the substring 'faq' in their names using the grep function: 

    
 opendir(FAQDIR,$faqdir)|| die "can't open $faqdir $!";
 my @faqs = grep /faq/,readdir FAQDIR;
 closedir FAQDIR;
