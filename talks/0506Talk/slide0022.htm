5.5 Quantifiers Woes

    Badly crafted nested quantifiers can have exponential runtime characteristics.
    Perl protects you from many of these, but be careful.
    Use more specific expressions to avoid backtracking. 
    
   # Which is more efficient?
   /"(.*)"/;
   /"([^"]+)"/;
    The second, less backtracking.
