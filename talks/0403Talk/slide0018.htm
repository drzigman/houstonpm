Summary Of Getopt::Long.

There are quite a few ways to configure the Getopt::Long module, including: ignoring case in options, passing through unknown options, allowing abbreviations (default), and changing the option-start character from '-' to something else. See the documentation for this module for further information (perldoc Getopt::Long).
