Boolean Options

The simplest kind of options to deal with are boolean options. Options that just turn on (or off) some particular behavior, or switch between two particular behaviors. In the faqgrep program, the -f option turns on the 'full-output' feature (causing matching entries to printed out in full, rather than just the headings). The -or option tells the program to treat multiple keywords as being logically OR'd together. That is, match any heading containing any of the search terms (the default behavior is to match headings containing all of the search terms given).
