2.1 The Common in the Module Name

    There are several common regular expression tasks that almost everyone has had to do.
    Most of them are not hard to get mostly right.
    Some of these are a little tricky to get completely right.
    If we look at regexes as little programs, it makes sense to reuse other people's code, rather than write it ourselves.
