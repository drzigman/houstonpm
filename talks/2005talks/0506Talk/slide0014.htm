4.4 Mismatches

    Wrong alternation 
    
   s/cat|dog|cattle/animal/;
    Anchors and alternation 
    
   # Wrong way to match a single command on a line.
   $line =~ /^START|STOP|RESTART|STATUS$/;

   # Better
   $line =~ /^(START|STOP|RESTART|STATUS)$/;
    Without parens, anchors only attach to first and last.
