7.6 Generic Quoting

You can use any non-whitespace, non-alphanumeric character as the delimiter. The delimiters must either be the same or a matching pair.

    q// - equivalent to single quotes
    qq// - equivalent to double quotes
    qx// - equivalent to back ticks
    qw// - creates a list from the whitespace-separated words
    qr// - compiles a regular expression
